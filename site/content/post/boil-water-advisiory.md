THE BOIL WATER ADVISORY has been issued  for the Irvine Municipal Utilities customers of the STUMP RD, AND CALMES RD AREA, to include, Raintree Court, Ruby Lane, Vada Drive, Calmes Road and Water Tower Lane.  Customers who live in these areas, until further notice, are asked to bring any water intended to be ingested to a rolling boil for 3 minutes.  Allow to water to cool before drinking.
