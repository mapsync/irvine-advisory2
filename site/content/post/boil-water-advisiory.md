A BOIL WATER ADVISORY has been issued for the Irvine Municipal Utilities customers of the WHITE OAK and STUMP to include, Oak Valley, Sunrise Valley, John Rawlins Road, Eades drive, McDowell Cemetery road, Dry Branch, Ruby Lane, Raintree Court, Duckware road, and Vada Drive.  Customers who live on those roads and drives, until further notice, are asked to bring any water intended to be ingested to a rolling boil for 3 minutes.  Allow to water to cool before drinking.
