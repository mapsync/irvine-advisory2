Once water has been restored a boil water advisory will be issued for the following area: ALL of Geneva Ave. Crews are on scene repairing the leak.

Customers under the boil water advisory using water for consumption will need to bring the water to a rolling boil for at least 3 minutes. For more info on boil water advisories please visit our website at imuwater.com.
