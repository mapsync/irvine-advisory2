Switching to paperless billing can offer several benefits, including convenience, timely delivery of bills, and cost savings on postage. Here's how you can take advantage of paperless billing:

1. Visit the website [www.imuwater.com](http://www.imuwater.com/).
2. Create an account if you haven't already.
3. Once logged in, navigate to the Manage My Settings section.
4. Look for the option to enroll in paperless billing.
5. Follow the prompts to set up your paperless billing preferences.

By opting for paperless billing, you'll receive your bills via email, allowing you to access them quickly and conveniently. This can help you stay on top of your payments and avoid late fees. Plus, it's an eco-friendly choice that reduces paper waste and helps conserve resources.
