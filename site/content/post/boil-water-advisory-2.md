**Boiled water advisory for the following areas has been lifted:**

\*Broadway St. from the main St. (Irvine) intersection, to the Lily Ave intersection.

\*Court St. from the Broadway intersection, to the River Drive intersection.

Customers living in this area will need to bring any tap water, to be consumed, to a rolling boil for 3 minutes. Allow water to cool before consumption.
