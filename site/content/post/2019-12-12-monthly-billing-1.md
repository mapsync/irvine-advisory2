**In order to avoid service disruptions,  balances must be paid IN FULL by the cut-off date.....NO EXCEPTIONS**

IMU water/sewer bills due on1st **14th**of every month.  If not paid by the 14th, a 10% penalty will be added to bill.   Cut off notices will be mailed on the 15th. If you have paid your bill online on the 14th please contact IMU during regular business hours.

Cut-off date for delinquent IMU water/sewer balances will be conducted on or after the 20th of the month.

Estill County Water District customers who have delinquent sewer bills with IMU are subject to cut-off also but cut-off date is determined by the Estill County Water District.
